Donald Duck

Donald Duck

The development of the Donald character began when Walt Disney heard the milkman Clarence Nash imitate animals in 1933, and animated Richard Lundy, Art Babbitt and Dick Huemer were commissioned to give the character shape. Donald made his debut in the short film The Wise Little Hen, which premiered on June 9, 1934. This is therefore considered Donald's official birthday, and he was thus able to celebrate his 80th birthday in June 2014.

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney was a filmmaker from the United States. He is without comparison the biggest name in the history of animated film and wins 29 Oscars.

In 1938, he founded the media and entertainment group Walt Disney Productions, today called the Walt Disney Company, in Burbank, California. It is one of the world's largest media groups and has film production, film distribution, broadcasting and the operation of amusement parks as its core business.

Disneyland

Disneyland

Disneyland is an amusement park in Anaheim, California, founded in 1954 by Walt Disney and opened in 1955.

Disneyland, located about 30 miles southeast of downtown Los Angeles, includes imaginative representations of fairy tales, American myth and history, exotic scenery, the depths of the ocean, Earth's past and the science fiction world of the future, totaling more than 90 different attractions, grouped in one share main themes (Adventureland, Fantasyland, Main Street USA, Tomorrowland, and more). The amusement park is visited annually by many millions of people.

Disneyland's success has led to the creation of several related amusement and theme parks


  • Walt Disney World in Orlando, Florida, opened in 1971.
  • Tokyo Disneyland in Urayasu, Japan, opened in 1983.
  • Disneyland Paris in Marne-la-Vallée, France, opened in 1992.
  • Hong Kong Disneyland opened on the island of Lantau in Hong Kong, China in 2005.
  • Shanghai Disneyland Park opened in Shanghai, China in 2016.
